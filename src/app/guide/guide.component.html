Guide

🎯 Objective

Ultimate Tic-Tac-Toe is a strategic twist on the classic game, using a grid of 9 mini tic-tac-toe boards arranged into a larger 3x3 board. The goal is to win three mini-boards in a row on the big board—just like regular tic-tac-toe, but with more depth.

📜 Rules Overview

  • Start: Player X goes first and can move in any cell of any mini-board.
  • Next Move Direction: The cell played sends the next player to the corresponding mini-board. For example, a move in the bottom-left cell sends the next player to the bottom-left board.
  • Mini-Board Wins: Players win a mini-board by aligning 3 of their marks. Won boards are locked.
  • Full/Won Mini-Boards: If a player is sent to a full or won board, they can move anywhere.
  • Game Victory: Win the game by winning 3 mini-boards in a line—horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.

⚖️ Tie-Breaker Rule

If all mini-boards are filled and no player has 3 in a row on the large board, the player who has won the most mini-boards wins the game.

If both players have an equal number of mini-board wins, the game ends in a draw.

🧠 Engine Features

  • Live Evaluations: The engine displays numerical evaluations for each legal move, giving insight into how strong each option is.
  • Step Navigation: Use arrow buttons to go backward and forward through the move history and explore alternate lines.

🔁 Recap

Ultimate Tic-Tac-Toe is simple to learn yet challenging to master. This engine makes it easier to explore positions, evaluate strategies, and enjoy this advanced variant of a classic favorite.
